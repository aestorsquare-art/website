Project Overview

Discover Aestor Square, an ultra-luxury gated residential community in Tusiyana, Knowledge Park-5, Noida Extension. Built on the principles of contemporary architecture and eco-conscious engineering, this premium project is developed by the highly acclaimed Lucky Homes Builders & Developers.

Aestor Square spans over a highly secure, beautifully landscaped 1800 sqyd Gated Housing Society, offering a peaceful and premium lifestyle environment. The project features two outstanding blocks, Samriddhi and Saumya, designed to cater to modern residential requirements with top-tier layouts and design optimization.

Every apartment is meticulously planned to maximize light, space utilization, and ventilation, providing a layout that feels extremely roomy and premium. Aestor Square delivers prime accessibility, putting major universities, IT zones, multi-specialty hospitals, and premium lifestyle malls in Noida Extension just a short drive away.
